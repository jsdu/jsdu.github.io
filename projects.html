Kingdoms Beyond is a passion project that I have been working on for the past 2 years. It is a blockchain MMORPG that leverages the Ethereum blockchain for tokenizing assets. The immediate benefits of using the blockchain as the central source of truth is that it empowers users. The assets truly belong to the users, and as such they can trade or sell their assets on the blockchain without the supervision of a game server.

Stake Away

Stake Away is a protocol that I built during the hackathon ETHDenver 2020. It was part of the larger project called Ledger Locker. This protocol allows any smart contract owner to earn interest off of their smart contract balance. The inspiration for this idea began when I was thinking about ways to monetize off of decentralized services. By making users earn money off interest, there is no fees between users using the services.

An example in a real world use case would be AirBnb. They usually charge 15% just to use their platform. A decentralized version of Airbnb may work where there is 0 fees between buyers and sellers, however there must be some way of bridging that gap between buyers and sellers as a service. Using Stake Away, a company can earn interest upwards of 8% at today's rates from Compound or DSR. This interest will be earned when potential users are currently renting a house until they leave.
